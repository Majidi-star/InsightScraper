**Cookies: The Unsung Heroes**

Cookies are small files stored on your browser that contain information about your preferences, device, or actions taken on a website. They help websites tailor content to individual users, providing a more personalized experience. Cookies can be broadly categorized into four types:

1. **Strictly Necessary Cookies**: Essential for website functionality, these cookies cannot be disabled without compromising the site's performance.
2. **Functional Cookies**: Enable enhanced features and personalization, but may not function properly if blocked.
3. **Performance Cookies**: Help websites analyze traffic, measure page performance, and improve user experience.
4. **Targeting Cookies**: Used by advertisers to build profiles of interests and display targeted ads.
